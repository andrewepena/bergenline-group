Who we are

Built on experience, driven by purpose.

Bergenline Group is an independent development and advisory studio focused on resilient urban projects. We bridge public and private partners to reimagine existing assets, unlock equitable investment, and deliver spaces that reflect the communities around them.

From feasibility through activation, our team leans on collaborative planning, rigorous analysis, and thoughtful stewardship to help clients move forward with confidence.

  • Urban infill, mixed-use, and public realm assignments
  • Cross-functional team with backgrounds in planning, finance, and placemaking
  • Emphasis on transparent communication and measurable community benefit
